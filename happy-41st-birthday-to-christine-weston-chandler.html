Happy 41st Birthday to Christine Weston Chandler

By InternetAmenhotep

Police Illustration of Chris, circa 2023


41 years ago Christopher Weston Chandler was born to his parents Bob

and Barb. Since then, he has undergone two name changes and one

gender change. Now, she goes by Christine Weston Chandler and is

currently residing in Central Virginia Regional Jail.


Over the past 20 years Chris has cemented himself as a staple of the

internet and become one of, if not the most well-documented humans

ever to have lived. As a child he was diagnosed with autism and went

through a yearslong period of muteness. Throughout high school he

was only friends with girls, all of whom his parents had an

arrangement with. In college he began pursuing a ‘love quest,’ in

which he held a sign in public and attempted to court female fellow

students. He was later expelled. He gradually began increasing his

internet presence by publishing his homemade original character

Sonichu's comic series. This attracted the attention of many online

trolls who would harass him throughout his life. In 2016, Chris

transitioned and became a real woman. Since then Chris's mental

health has been on a downward spiral due to nasty trolls. In 2021

Chris admitted to having sex with his mother and was arrested. He

has been held in prison ever since.


Some might say Chris's story is sick or twisted. And it is. But I believe

that it is still an inspirational tale. We, just like Chris, should strive for

our dreams no matter what, even if the whole world is against us.

Never stop trying. Never back down and ride the tiger.
